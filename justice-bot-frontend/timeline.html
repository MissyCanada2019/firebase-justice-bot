Your Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Form Generation

Your LTB T2 form has been generated based on the information you provided. You can download it from your case file.

Step 2: Filing with the LTB

File the generated form with the Landlord and Tenant Board. This can be done online, by mail, or in person. Ensure you have paid the filing fee.

Step 3: Serving Documents

You must serve the other party (your landlord) with a copy of the filed application. Proof of service is required.

Step 4: Hearing Preparation

Organize your evidence, prepare your arguments, and arrange for any witnesses. A hearing date will be scheduled by the LTB.
