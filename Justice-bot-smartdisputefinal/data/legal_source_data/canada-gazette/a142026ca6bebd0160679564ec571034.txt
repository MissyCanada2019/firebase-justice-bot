Canada Gazette, Part II: Volume 141
Browse and read editions of the Canada Gazette, Part II. This part contains all enacted regulations and statutory instruments. Regular editions are published every second Wednesday and are available online at 9 am eastern time.
The Canada Gazette Directorate provides both the HTML and PDF versions of the Canada Gazette for the last 5 years. Earlier editions, such as those found on this page, are only available in the PDF version.
