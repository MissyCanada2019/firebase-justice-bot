About Colm Brannigan
Colm Brannigan is an ADR practitioner based in Ontario, with a practice range spanning various subject areas in dispute resolution. A recognized leader in online dispute resolution (ODR) and the use of Med-Arb as a stand-alone process, he is particularly focused on the integration of technology, including artificial intelligence, in ADR processes. Colm holds an LL.M. in ADR and several professional designations, including that of Chartered Med-Arbitrator through the ADR Institute of Canada.
