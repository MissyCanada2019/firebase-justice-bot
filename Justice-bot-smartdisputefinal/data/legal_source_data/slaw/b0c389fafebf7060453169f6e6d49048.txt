About AALL Spectrum
The American Association of Law Libraries (AALL) bimonthly member magazine, AALL Spectrum, is designed to further professional development and education within the legal information industry. The intent of the magazine is to identify emerging trends and best practices within legal information that will support the work of legal information professionals. Each article aims to give readers takeaways that can be implemented in their organizations to improve processes, to work more efficiently, and to grow their professional development. AALL Spectrum articles are written by AALL members and nonmembers.
