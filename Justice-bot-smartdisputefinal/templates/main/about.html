The Story Behind Smart Dispute Canada

WHO: Teresa Bertin - A Canadian Mom Fighting for Justice

I'm Teresa, a full-time mother from Ontario who learned firsthand how broken our legal system is when only the wealthy can afford representation. After winning multiple self-represented cases, I decided no Canadian should face court alone.

WHAT: SmartDispute.ai - Real Legal Empowerment

SmartDispute.ai is a comprehensive AI-powered platform that analyzes your evidence, determines legal pathways, and generates court-ready documents. It's not just information - it's complete legal self-empowerment that equals the court system.

WHEN: Built Through Years of Experience

After years of self-representation in family court, fighting CAS, and navigating employment disputes, I partnered with Canadian AI developers in 2024 to create this platform. We're launching with 1000 free pilot users before transitioning to paid services.

WHERE: Made in Canada, For All Canadians

Based in Ontario but serving all of Canada, SmartDispute.ai covers federal, provincial, and municipal laws across all provinces and territories. Available 24/7 online to ensure justice never sleeps.

WHY: Because Justice Shouldn't Be a Luxury

I created this because I've seen too many good people lose everything simply because they couldn't afford a lawyer. Courts should be places of "real fairness and justice, not a game of money and privilege." Every Canadian deserves equal access to justice, regardless of income.

As a pilot user, you get unlimited access to help us perfect the platform for all Canadians.

  • AI analysis of all evidence (documents, photos, conversations)
